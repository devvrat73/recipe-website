Classic Vegetable Biryani

Prep Time: 45 minutes Difficulty: Medium Type: Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves, 2 cardamom, 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish
  • Saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Then parboil it with salt, bay leaf, and whole spices. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pan. Add cumin seeds, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon. Let them splutter.
  3. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until the raw smell goes away.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  6. Add the mixed vegetables, yogurt, red chili powder, and biryani masala. Mix well and cook for 5-6 minutes.
  7. Layer the parboiled rice over the vegetable mixture. Add mint leaves and coriander on top.
  8. If using saffron milk, drizzle it over the rice for colour and flavour.
  9. Cover the pan tightly with a lid and cook on low flame (dum) for about 20 minutes.
  10. Gently mix and serve hot with raita or salad.
