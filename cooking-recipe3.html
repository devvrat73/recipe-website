Masala Maggi Noodles

Prep Time: 10 minutes Difficulty: Easy Type: Snack

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of Maggi noodles (with masala tastemaker)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 capsicum, chopped
  • 1/4 cup peas (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander for topping

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them crackle for a few seconds.
  2. Add chopped onions and fry until they turn slightly golden.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook for 2 minutes till they become soft.
  4. Add capsicum, peas, turmeric, and red chili powder. Stir and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Pour 2 cups of water into the pan and bring it to a boil.
  6. Add the Maggi noodles and both the masala tastemaker packets.
  7. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, stirring in between so the noodles don't stick.
  8. Adjust salt if needed. Cook till the water is mostly absorbed but it's still a little saucy.
  9. Turn off the flame and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot immediately. Maggi gets soggy if you leave it too long!
