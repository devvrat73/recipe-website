Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 1 hour Difficulty: Medium Type: Dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder (unsweetened)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup oil (any neutral oil)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • For frosting: 100g dark chocolate + 1/2 cup cream

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C. Grease a round cake tin with oil and dust with flour.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Sift well.
  3. In another bowl, whisk eggs, oil, milk, and vanilla essence together.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix gently with a spatula. Do not overmix.
  5. Add the hot water slowly and stir until the batter becomes smooth. The batter will look quite thin – that is normal.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake tin and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  7. Check if done by inserting a toothpick in the center – it should come out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool completely before removing from the tin.
  9. For frosting: heat cream in a saucepan, pour over chopped dark chocolate, and stir until smooth. Let it cool a bit.
  10. Pour the chocolate frosting over the cooled cake and spread evenly. Slice and serve!
