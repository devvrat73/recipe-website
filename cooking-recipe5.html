Gulab Jamun

Prep Time: 40 minutes Difficulty: Hard Type: Dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder (full fat)
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee (softened)
  • 3-4 tbsp milk (to knead dough)
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying
  • For sugar syrup:
  • 1 and 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 4-5 cardamom pods (crushed)
  • 1 tsp rose water
  • A few saffron strands (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. First make the sugar syrup: boil sugar and water together, stirring until sugar dissolves. Add cardamom and rose water. Keep it on low heat until it becomes slightly sticky. Turn off and keep warm.
  2. In a bowl, mix milk powder, maida, and baking soda together.
  3. Add softened ghee and rub it into the flour mixture with your fingers until it resembles breadcrumbs.
  4. Add milk little by little and gently knead into a soft dough. Do not over-knead. Let it rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into small equal portions and roll each into a smooth ball (no cracks). Make them small as they will double in size after frying.
  6. Heat oil/ghee in a deep pan on low-medium flame. The oil should not be too hot – this is very important!
  7. Carefully slide in the balls and fry on low heat, turning gently, until they become dark golden brown all over. This takes around 10-12 minutes on low heat.
  8. Remove and immediately drop them into the warm sugar syrup.
  9. Let them soak in the syrup for at least 30 minutes before serving. The longer they soak, the softer they get.
  10. Serve warm or at room temperature, garnished with a pinch of pistachio powder if you like.
