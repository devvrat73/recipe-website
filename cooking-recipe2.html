Paneer Butter Masala

Prep Time: 30 minutes Difficulty: Medium Type: Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 3 tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 2 onions, roughly chopped
  • 10-12 cashews
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt and sugar to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil onions, tomatoes, and cashews together with a little water for 10 minutes. Let it cool, then blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add the blended paste to the pan. Cook on medium flame for about 8-10 minutes, stirring regularly.
  4. Add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for another 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add a little water if the gravy looks too thick. Bring it to a gentle simmer.
  6. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes so the paneer absorbs the flavours.
  7. Pour in the fresh cream and mix gently. Do not boil after adding cream.
  8. Crush kasuri methi and sprinkle on top. Add garam masala and a pinch of sugar.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves and a dollop of butter on top.
  10. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
